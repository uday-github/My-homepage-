Bio

I am Uday Khandelwal. I graduated from UC San Diego with my MS in 2014 majoring in Signal & Image Processing which is a field of Electrical Engineering. My research interests are mainly in data processing and development of DSP algortihms with application in areas of machine learning, magnetic recording, sports science to name a few. I received my B.E degree from Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), Manipal in India in 2008 in Electronics and Communication Engg.

I was born in the majestic city of Jaipur in the state of Rajasthan in India on October 12, 1990. After 18 years of education in my city at my almameter Maharaja Sawai Mansingh Vidyalaya, I came much down south to the town of rains and nice people at Manipal in the Dakshin Kannada regionto pursue my bachelors. After 4 years and a great time with many pals in Manipal , I decided to go on an odyssey much far west to the United States in at UC San Diego in San Diego, California. 3 years and I am here, let's see what places does life take me from here on.
